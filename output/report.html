Incoming Clause

Playbook Clause

Nothing in this Agreement or in any document referred to in it shall constitute any Party a partner of the other, nor shall the execution, completion and implementation of this DPA confer on any Party any power to bind or impose any obligation on the other Party.

Explanation

The clause establishes the DPA as integral to the main agreement, which conflicts with ClearTax's playbook requirement that the DPA be entered into on a principal-to-principal basis. This creates potential ambiguity about the relationship structure and governing terms.
